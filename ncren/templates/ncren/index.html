Welcome to ncREnvironment

ncREnvironment is a database that curates experiment-supported evidence for non-coding RNAs [including microRNAs (miRNAs), long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) and circular RNAs (circRNAs)], environmental factors and their associated phenotypes, and is the updated version of miREnvironment ( http://www.cuilab.cn/miren) which were released on June 28, 2011. Through manual curation of over 12,000 papers, a more than five-fold growth of entries has been achieved in comparison with previous database, which contains a comprehensive collection and curation of experiment-supported evidence among miRNAs, lncRNAs, circRNAs, environmental factors and phenotypes and represents a biomedical resource for researches on ncRNAs, environmental factors and diseases.
Statistics:

Currently, ncREnvironment integrated 24274 entries from 12091 publications, involving 3956 miRNAs, 1320 lncRNAs, 565 circRNAs, 3157 environmental factors, 2781 phenotypes, and 82 species.

History:

June 26, 2021, ncREnvironment Database was released.

    What is ncREnvironment

    ncREnvironment is a database that curate and collect experiment-supported non-coding RNAs and environmental factors (EFs) and their associated phenotypes. Currently, ncREnvironment integrated 24276 entries from 12091 publications, involving 3956 miRNAs, 1320 lncRNAs, 565 circRNAs, 3157 environmental factors, 2781 phenotypes, and 82 species. In the database, ncREnvironment database provides the function of browse, search and download for users.

    How to use nvREnvironment

    Browse

    The browse page consists of three parts:

    (1). The directory tree in the left panel, users can click '+' button to view the detailed classifications or entries. Non-coding RNAs mainly include miRNAs, lncRNAs and circRNAs. As for species, it mainly shows human, mouse, rat and other species.

    (2). In the right panel, we show the ncRNA-EF-phenotype list that users are interested in. Besides, users can click the "+" button in the "Detail" column to view the detailed information, such as PubMed ID, Species, Sample, Condition of EF and Evidence.

    Search

    In the search page, we provide an advanced search capability, users can obtain the ncRNA-EF-Phenotype associations by enter one or multiple keywords, such as ncRNA name, environmental factor name, species and their combination. ncREnvironment will return fuzzy matching results.

    Result

    In the search result panel, users can view the ncRNA-EF-phenotype list of interests.

    More information about the entry is also displayed while users click the "+" button in 'Detail' column.

    Download

    To download data in the database, click the “Download” menu. ncREnvironment provides two formats of downloadable file for the whole data in Text and Excel formats, respectively. Moreover, files for the lists of miRNAs, lncRNAs, circRNAs, environmental factors, and phenotypes are also given.
